Why You Need to Check Your Personality

In the present culture, where time is considered the essence, there has never been a more increased emphasis on a person's overall development. While we invest countless hours in enhancing our career skills, physical well-being, and social relationships, there is one domain that is overlooked – personality. Understanding oneself as a person, what one does well, what one does not do well, what one likes, and what one dislikes can be the most valuable asset in a person’s self-improvement and organization’s benefit. This is where personality assessments come in handy, as they provide an objective way of approaching one’s personality. Self-awareness is the key to growth, and at InnerLens, we embrace this belief. This is why you should consider finding out about your personality and how InnerLens might help in that process.

1. Self-Awareness is the Foundation of Personal Growth

In any path of self-improvement process, one must determine his or her starting point. Self-knowledge is useful in determining strengths and doing a self-stock take on areas one can enhance. Most of the time, people have a general idea about themselves but those ideas are usually based on assumptions, societal norms, or lack of information. A formal and properly developed personality assessment such as InnerLens can help to be more ordered and less subjectively determine who you really are.

For instance, while you may think you are an introvert, an assessment exercise may show that you are an extrovert but in a particular context. It is possible that the person assumes he or she is, for instance, very much a strategist when, in fact, he or she is rather creative according to the test taken. These perceptions can revolutionize one’s worldview.

2. Unlock Your Full Potential

The first reason why one should check his/her personality is to optimize his/her potential. Knowing your personal strengths lets you build on them while at the same time improving your areas of shortcomings. InnerLens provides in-depth reports on the different aspects of a user and on how to make the most of that score.

Now, let’s assume that problem-solving is your natural strength, even if you are not very good at emotional intelligence. An elaborate personality profile should encompass appropriate interpersonal behaviors on how to relate better and perform better depending on the workplace environment.

3. Make Better Career Decisions

From the analysis made here, we can conclude that personality plays a vital role when it comes to employment, career, and job satisfaction. Whenever candidates follow their personalities and choose specific careers, they are usually happier, and their performance improves also. Thus, this is to say that having a formal definite idea about one’s personality, whether as a novice joining a workforce or a career changer, or even an employee who wants to make improvements in working conditions, self-understanding is central.

For instance, an employee with high introversion and order would like to work in positions requiring a lot of individual work and procedural jobs like analysis or being a writer. On the other hand, an extroverted, spontaneous person will be pleased dealing with numerous interactions and constant meetings in a sales or marketing job. The InnerLens method in the assessment of your personality will provide you with the best career paths for your personality.

4. Improve Your Relationships

These elements also make personality assessments valuable to you, especially in matters relating to your personal life. When it comes to others, it is always easier to deal with them and avoid conflicts if you know their personalities: it can be friends, family members, or even partners. Once one understands his/her traits as well as those of others, one can control temperance, prevent disagreements, and work towards understanding horizons.

InnerLens is a tool that offers information on how you engage with people and what environment best suits your skills. By applying it in your day-to-day life, you will be able to avoid common conflicts that can appear in relationships and start seeing things that you or your partner may change.

5. Enhance Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence is therefore defined as the skill that enables one to recognize and regulate his own emotions and those of the people around him. It is one of the skills that can seriously upgrade your life in practically any aspect of it from work to personal life. A good EQ enables a person to manage relationships, conflicting situations, and establish productive and healthy relationships with other people.

This makes it possible for anyone to know how emotionally intelligent he/she is and where he/she is lacking by taking a personality assessment such as InnerLens. For example, one can be an introvert who is considerate and caring but does not like to be aggressive or pushy.

It can also help to recognize your personality if it is going to help in the process of enhancing your mental health. A person who has well-defined knowledge of personality characteristics will be in a better position to manage stress, decision making, and promote balance in their way of living. It therefore becomes easy to act towards the prevention of stress or anxiety-related conditions based on triggers that you may come across.

Personality is not static. It changes with the features of life events, the tasks, and the attempts at self-improvement.
